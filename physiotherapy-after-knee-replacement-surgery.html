Blog Series: Post-Operative Physiotherapy at Mittal’s Physiofit

    person Dr. Sachendra Mittal calendar_today 31 July, 2025

Expert Rehabilitation for Post-Surgical Recovery

At Mittal’s Physiofit Clinic, Jaipur, our blog series on post-operative physiotherapy is designed to guide patients through tailored rehabilitation for various surgeries. Each post provides insights into specific challenges, physiotherapy’s role in recovery, and practical tips to ensure a smooth return to daily life.

1. Physiotherapy After Knee Replacement Surgery

Common Challenges: Stiffness, difficulty walking, weak thigh muscles

  • Role of physiotherapy in early mobilization, strengthening, and gait training
  • Home exercises and do’s & don’ts after knee replacement

2. Physiotherapy After Hip Replacement Surgery

Key Focus: Safe recovery to regain mobility

  • Importance of safe movements to avoid dislocation
  • Strengthening hip and pelvic muscles
  • Functional training for sitting, standing, and stair climbing

3. Physiotherapy After Spine Surgery

Key Focus: Restoring spinal function

  • Posture correction and spinal mobility exercises
  • Core strengthening for spinal stability
  • Ergonomic advice for daily activities

4. Physiotherapy After Fracture Fixation (Plates, Rods, Screws)

Key Challenges: Overcoming stiffness and weakness

  • Managing stiffness and scar tissue
  • Gradual return to normal movement
  • Balance and strength recovery

5. Physiotherapy After Cardiac Surgery (Cardiac Rehab)

Key Focus: Enhancing heart and lung function

  • Breathing exercises to improve lung capacity
  • Gentle strengthening to improve endurance
  • Lifestyle and stress management guidance

6. Physiotherapy After Neurological Surgeries

Key Focus: Restoring neurological function

  • Regaining muscle control and coordination
  • Balance and gait training
  • Functional independence exercises

Why This Blog Series Matters

Each surgery presents unique challenges, and our dedicated blog series ensures patients find targeted guidance for their specific needs. By providing expert advice, Mittal’s Physiofit Clinic establishes itself as a trusted rehabilitation center, helping patients recover confidently and efficiently.
