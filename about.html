About US

Welcome to Mittal’s Physiofit – A Hub of Rehabilitation

Established in 2019, Mittal’s Physiofit stands as a trusted center for holistic healing and physical wellness. Founded by Dr. Leena and Dr. Sachendra Mittal, the clinic is the realization of their two decades of dedication to the science of physiotherapy and the art of compassionate care.

At Mittal’s Physiofit, we blend medical expertise with a deeply human touch. Our team of experienced and certified physiotherapists is committed not just to treating symptoms, but to understanding and addressing the root causes of each condition. We recognize that every patient is a person first — navigating physical, emotional, social, or even economic challenges — and not just a medical case.

What sets us apart is our patient-centered approach. With skilled hands and caring hearts, our therapists deliver personalized care that combines scientific precision with empathetic support. We believe that true rehabilitation goes beyond techniques and technology — it lies in a genuine connection with those we serve.

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or simply working towards better physical health, Mittal’s Physiofit is here to guide you every step of the way — helping you move better, live stronger, and heal naturally.

About Me

Dr. Leena Mittal is a dynamic and compassionate neuro-physiotherapist, co-founder of Mittal’s Physiofit, and a highly respected figure in the field of Neurological Rehabilitation. With over 15 years of clinical experience, she specializes in treating a wide spectrum of neurological conditions, including stroke, cerebral palsy, spinal cord injuries, Parkinson’s disease, and other neuro-motor disorders.

Her academic and professional journey is marked by a deep commitment to continuous learning and integrative care. She is CNDT (Certified Neuro-Developmental Therapist) and holds multiple certifications that enhance her holistic approach — including Aerobic & Power Yoga training and Maternal Health Care under IAFT. These additional qualifications enable her to blend conventional neurorehabilitation with wellness practices that improve overall quality of life, especially for women during pre- and postnatal phases.

Dr. Leena is a firm believer in patient-centred care, where emotional support and motivational counselling go hand-in-hand with evidence-based therapy. Her sessions are thoughtfully crafted to restore confidence, mobility, and independence in her patients — often becoming a transformative journey for them and their families.

She is also an active life member of MIAP (Indian Association of Physiotherapists) and MIAFT (Indian Association of Fitness Trainers), reflecting her dedication to professional excellence and ethical practice.

As the Chief Consultant at Mittal’s Physiofit, she plays a pivotal role in mentoring junior therapists, developing rehabilitation protocols, and ensuring every patient experiences healing with empathy, expertise, and encouragement.

Dr. Sachendra Mittal

Co-Founder, Mittal’s Physiofit – A Hub of Rehabilitation & Mittal’s Medical Classes BPT, M.Sc., Ph.D. (Anatomy), MBA (Hospital Administration)

About Me

Dr. Sachendra Mittal is the visionary founder and driving force behind Mittal’s Physiofit, combining over 20 years of medical experience with a passion for patient-centred care. A highly respected academician and healthcare leader, Dr. Mittal holds advanced qualifications in Anatomy and Hospital Administration, giving him a unique perspective on both the science of the human body and the systems that support healing.

His deep-rooted belief in integrative, empathetic, and evidence-based rehabilitation led to the establishment of Mittal’s Physiofit in 2019. Since then, the clinic has become a trusted name in physiotherapy and rehabilitation in Jaipur and beyond. Under his leadership, the clinic emphasizes not just treatment, but transformative healing — where each patient is treated with compassion, respect, and clinical precision.

Dr. Mittal is also a well-known educator in the field of Anatomy and medical sciences, having mentored numerous healthcare professionals. His ability to connect academic insight with practical patient care is what truly sets him apart.

With his commitment to excellence and innovation, Dr. Sachendra Mittal continues to lead the Mittal’s Physiofit team in delivering holistic wellness solutions that empower patients to regain movement, independence, and quality of life.
